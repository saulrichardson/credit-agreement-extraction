## Accession Narratives

### 0001819974-23-000003 – SkyWater Technology, Inc.
- EX-10.1 is the full Loan and Security Agreement with Siena Lending; the tiered grid appears in Schedule B under the definition of “Applicable Margin,” which ties the Term SOFR/Base Rate margins to the Fixed Charge Coverage Ratio (projects/credit-agreement-extraction/scratch/task06/0001819974-23-000003_EX-10.1.txt:1988-2008).
- Borrowers that miss the reporting deadlines in Schedule D or are in default are automatically bumped to the highest Applicable Margin until cure, and any later restatement can retroactively increase the paid margin (id.:2010-2026).

### 0000950170-23-000122 – Lumen Technologies, Inc.
- The amended and restated credit agreement (EX-10.1) uses leverage-based pricing in the Section 1.01 definition of “Applicable Rate,” with separate grids for periods before the Second Amendment, between amendments, and after the Revolving Facility Amendment Date (projects/credit-agreement-extraction/scratch/task06/0000950170-23-000122_EX-10.1.txt:7960-8150).
- Commitment fees follow the same leverage tiers in Section 2.09(a), and any missed compliance certificate or continuing Event of Default automatically applies the highest margin/fee level until a compliant certificate is delivered (id.:8160-8348).

### 0001140361-23-000246 – Kimco Realty Corporation
- EX-10.1 (Amended and Restated Credit Agreement) defines “Applicable Margin” by “Status,” which is driven by Kimco’s unsecured debt ratings (projects/credit-agreement-extraction/scratch/task06/0001140361-23-000246_EX-10.1.txt:3780-4176, 7680-7794).
- The Sustainability Metric Grid allows a 1 bp reduction (never below zero) once Kimco delivers the ESG notice showing the target was met, and similar grids govern the Facility Fee Rate and L/C Fee Rate; failure to deliver financials or a downgrade drives the Status level higher per the same section.

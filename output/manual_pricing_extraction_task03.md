## Accession Narratives
### 0001140361-23-000046 – Kimco Realty Corp. Revolving Credit Facility
- Located the EX-10.1 credit agreement (`ny20006479x6_ex10-1.htm`) inside `data/daily_filings/2023/QTR1/20230103/0001140361-23-000046.nc` by searching for “Applicable Margin”/“Schedule 1.1”. The definition section embeds multiple grids that set the Applicable Margin, Facility Fee Rate, and Letter of Credit Fee Rate by “Status”.
- Status levels are tied to Kimco’s unsecured debt ratings (Level I: A/A2 or better; Level VI: below BBB-/Baa3) with split-rating mechanics (higher rating governs unless two-step gap, in which case average of two highest ratings) and the option to incorporate a third agency.
- A Sustainability Metric Notice can temporarily replace the standard grid with the Sustainability Metric Grid (Tables 13 and 16) for 370 days so long as the reported Sustainability Metric is at/below the target. If no new notice arrives, pricing reverts to the base grid.
- The same tier labels are reused for facility fees and standby LC fees, so every status requires capturing each column (Term Benchmark/RFR/Money Market margin, ABR/Canadian Prime margin, Facility Fee Rate, LC Fee Rate).
- Footnotes to the Status definition also state that late financial statements default the Applicable Margin, Facility Fee Rate, and LC Fee Rate to the lowest status until compliance; this is noted in the narrative for quality control.

### 0001193125-23-000306 – DNOW Inc. Asset-Based Revolver
- Reviewed the Second Amendment to Credit Agreement (`d407158dex101.htm`) within `data/daily_filings/2023/QTR1/20230103/0001193125-23-000306.nc`. The “Applicable Margin” definition carries a three-level Fixed Charge Coverage Ratio grid with separate Base Rate and Non-Base Rate columns.
- The amendment reiterates that margins reset on the first day of the month after the ratio certificate is delivered; if the certificate is late or inaccurate, pricing snaps to Level III until corrected and the borrower must true-up unpaid interest.
- The “Applicable Unused Line Fee Percentage” uses a utilization grid (Average Revolver Usage relative to the Maximum Revolver Amount) that still sits in the definition section. Events of Default and the historical First Amendment period force the unused fee to Level II, which is documented as part of the notes/footnotes.
- No other tiered schedule was present; interest rate floors and SOFR definitions were fixed amounts, so only the margin and unused fee grids were extracted.
